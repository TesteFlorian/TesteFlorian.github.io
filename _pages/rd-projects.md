## Agentic Analytics for Analysts

- Build retrieval-augmented LLM agents that orchestrate Model Context Protocol tools across text, imagery, and geospatial APIs.
- Equip analysts with structured outputs, traceable citations, and automated GEOINT reporting workflows.
- Evaluate agent performance with task harnesses that measure accuracy, latency, and trust metrics.

## Food Security Forecasting

- Develop pipelines that convert multi-terabyte satellite streams into early-season yield and price forecasts for global commodities.
- Explore self-supervised feature extraction (AEs, VAEs, contrastive learning) to capture phenological signals months before harvest.
- Publish open methodologies enabling governments and NGOs to plan interventions with weeks of additional lead time.

## Environmental Monitoring & Forestry

- Fuse LiDAR and Sentinel imagery to estimate forest structural attributes for climate reporting and sustainable management.
- Detect floods, vegetation stress, and land-use change with multimodal data and domain-adapted neural architectures.
- Collaborate with R&D labs and industry partners to translate prototypes into production services.

## Current Experiments

- Scaling EO foundation models with adaptive fine-tuning for maritime anomaly detection.
- Extending agentic pipelines with continuous learning loops and human-in-the-loop validation.
- Benchmarking probabilistic deep learning approaches for commodity market forecasting under data drift.
